Has handled a substantial volume of litigation (criminal and civil), commercial arbitration, and labor arbitration cases, covering stages including first instance trial, second instance trial, and enforcement (including but not limited to enforcement objections and enforcement reconsideration), as well as labor arbitration and commercial arbitration. Skilled in dispute resolution across criminal defense, labor disputes, contracts, family matters, commercial affairs, finance, intellectual property, and unfair competition.
